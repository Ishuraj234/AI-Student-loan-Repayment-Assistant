About FinanceWise

We're on a mission to democratize financial knowledge and empower every individual to make smarter financial decisions.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, FinanceWise was born from a simple observation: financial literacy is a critical life skill, yet it remains inaccessible to many. Our founders, a team of finance professionals and technology experts, witnessed firsthand how the lack of financial education affected people's lives and futures.

What started as a small project to help friends and family understand their loan options has evolved into a comprehensive platform that leverages AI to provide personalized financial guidance to thousands of users across India.

2020

The Beginning

FinanceWise was founded with the mission to simplify financial decisions for everyone.

2021

AI Integration

Launched our first AI-powered financial assistant to provide personalized guidance.

2022

Expanding Services

Added comprehensive loan analysis tools and expanded to serve users across all major Indian cities.

2023

Today

Serving over 100,000 users with advanced financial tools and personalized guidance.
